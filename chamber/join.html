Membership Benefits

Non-Profit Membership

1. Access to the Coruscant Chamber of Commerce information guide, including upcoming and previous events and pricing

2. Information on what to do in case of an emergency, or an invasion happening on Coruscant

Bronze Membership

1. Access to All Non-Profit Benefits

2. One free ticket to any event on Coruscant

3. Prioty Seating at events

Silver Membership

1. Access to all Non-Profit and Bronze Benefits

2. Priority Jedi Youngling training for your children and family

3. Even more access to information, events, history, and the market, including the best places in your area to shop

Gold Membership

1. Access to all Non-Profit, Bronze, and Silver Benefits

2. Free parking at events

3. Intel on specific happenings in the clone wars, and the workings of the galactic republic

4. Increased priority seating for all events, and increased priority training for younglings

5. Free ticket to a total of three events throughout the year
