Site Name: Coruscant Galactic City Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The Galactic City of Coruscant Chamber of Commerce Website is here to provide the best information available for all of your business and informational needs and concerns. We provide you with the best resources possible in order to facilitate the growth of not only the economy of the Galactic City, but of understanding for all those who wish to tour the wonderful planet, or for anyone seeking to know the history behind the Galactic City, including all the wars and politics that Coruscant has been involved in. No matter what galaxy or planet you're from, you're guaranteed to gain increased knowledge and awareness about the wonderful Galactic City!

Scenarios

1. Culture and Tourism: How many species live in the Galactic City of Coruscant, and what are they? What is the culture like?

2. History and Politics: What is the history of the Galactic City, including it's many battles and politics?

3. Trading and Economy: What is the economy like in the Galactic City? How popular of a trade route does it have, and what kind of trading goes on there?
